eXist-db & Java Service Wrapper
===============================

Out of the box the by eXist-db provided 'java service wrapper' supports 
the following mainstream platforms:

- Windows x86 (32bit/64bit)
- Linux x86 (32bit/64bit) & IA (64bit)
- Mac OS X x86 (32bit/64bit)
- Solaris x86 (32bit/64bit) & SPARC (32bit/64bit)

Support for additional platforms can be obtained by downloading the "Delta Pack" 
from the "Tanuki software" website. (http://wrapper.tanukisoftware.com)

Available platforms are: AIX (PPC), FreeBSD (x86), HPUX and Linux (PPC,ARM).
